Description: Guitar Tabber is our website application that converts the notes you play on your guitar, into the tableture that you can read qand repeat. This is useful because now you can play a soong and keep the tabs so that you can play it later without completely forgetting the song. Or, you could hand out the tableture you create to friends so they can read your music themselves. Guitar tabber works by recieving the audio that you play, converts the hertz into a numerical amount, and use functions to turn the amounts into the digits in their designated tabs. Our program is original and one-of-a-kind, and has the ability to expand to other instruments.
